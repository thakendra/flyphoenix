About FlyPhoenix

Nepal's Premier Study Abroad Consultancy

Established in Bagbazzar, Kathmandu, FlyPhoenix has been Nepal's most trusted educational consultancy for over a decade. We help students navigate the complex process of studying abroad — from choosing the right university to landing safely at your destination.

Our team of experienced counselors provides honest, personalised guidance — because your future is too important for one-size-fits-all advice.

  • Personalised university matching & application support
  • Expert visa documentation with 95%+ approval rate
  • IELTS, PTE, TOEFL, SAT & GMAT preparation classes
  • Scholarship identification & application guidance
  • Pre-departure sessions & post-arrival support
