Our Origin Story

Born from a Student's Own Struggle

FlyPhoenix was founded in 2014 in the heart of Bagbazar, Kathmandu — not from a boardroom strategy, but from a personal experience of how hard it is to navigate the world of study abroad without honest guidance.

Our founder Vijay Vishwakarma experienced the confusion, the wrong advice, the wasted time first-hand. He built FlyPhoenix so that no Nepali student would ever have to walk through that fog alone.

Today, with over 500 students placed across 8+ countries, FlyPhoenix stands as Nepal's most trusted educational consultancy — driven not by commission, but by a genuine commitment to every student's future.

"Every student who walks through our door deserves the honest, personal guidance I wish I had. Your dream is my responsibility."

— Vijay Vishwakarma, Founder & CEO

Our Core Values

What Makes Us Different

These are not just words on a wall — they are the principles that guide every conversation, every application, every visa file.

Radical Honesty

We tell you what is genuinely right for your profile — not what earns us the highest commission. If a country isn't right for you, we'll say so.

Student-First Culture

Every decision we make is measured by one question: Is this the best thing for this student? Your success is our only KPI.

Zero Shortcuts

We never advise misrepresentation. Every document, every statement is crafted honestly — because your long-term future matters more than shortcuts.

Lifelong Relationship

Our job doesn't end when you board the flight. FlyPhoenix alumni can always reach out — we're here for you even years after your arrival.

Expert Knowledge

Our counselors continuously update themselves on visa rules, university requirements, and scholarship changes so you always receive accurate, current advice.

Personal Attention

We deliberately keep batch sizes small. Every student is a person with a unique story — not a number in a spreadsheet. You deserve one-on-one care.
